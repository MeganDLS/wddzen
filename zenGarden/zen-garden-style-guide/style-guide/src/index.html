Color Zen Garden Style Guide

Purpose/Theme

Our purpose is to show a colorful and dynamic layout that can showcase the power of CSS. We wish to add to the Zen Garden motive to “…excite, inspire, and encourage participation”. We want to show how even novices of CSS can have total control over every element of their hypertext document and show their vision of beauty of CSS design.

Typography

Headings

Headings Font in Lora. Regular paragraph font will be a sans-serif Poppins becuase Sans-serif is easier to read on various devices in different sizes. We have chosen a third font to differenciate between the paragraph font and links. We're using Concert One block font for links. We want links to be easily identified and do not want to use competing fonts.
